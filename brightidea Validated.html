Welcome to Bright Idea

Bright Idea is a family-owned business that has been serving the needs of entrepreneurs and innovators for more than forty years. Our first "bright idea" was to gather together pioneering individuals who wanted to share their thoughts, get feedback on new ideas, and help other inventive people.

We advise clients on a wide range of creative endeavors. We have over 200 patents registered today with another 20 in process. Our clients have created new and innovative businesses, services, tools, safety devices, and engineering models.

  • Innovative ideas
  • Entrepreneurship efforts
